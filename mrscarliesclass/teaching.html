We encourage the children to think creativetly at the school. Being able to think creatively is something that I believe is important. Every kid is an artist, and going outside the lines is encouraged because thinking outside the box is important.

Math is something that children often struggle with. At my school we focus on counting and adding and a little bit of subtraction (within 5-10). Math isnt just worksheets its counting the blocks at playtime or creating fun patterns.

Science at this school will always be something to look forward too. I believe in letting the kids think and become their own little sciencetists, their isn't one thing in science that could be wrong. If the kids ar curious I will let them live out their wildest dreams.
